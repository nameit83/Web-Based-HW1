Hero Alom is a renowned Dota 2 player in the US East region. Originally from Ason Chowk, Nepal Hero has made a reputation for himself as one of the most stubborn carries in the game. His toxic behavior towards his teammates combined with endless flaming of his opponents almost always ensures victory for him in a dota game. Although he has never openly admitted it, there has been a widespread rumor that he is a huge fan of s2.



Hero Alom initially started playing dota to help his best friend forever (BFF) s2 improve on the game
